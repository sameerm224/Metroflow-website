Reconciliation

Audit trail for business metrics

When a KPI moves 8% week-over-week, stakeholders need more than a chart — they need provenance. Metroflow records every definition change, upstream model deploy, and data delay that could explain the shift.

Reconciliation views compare certified metrics against source-of-record systems (Stripe, Salesforce, ERP) and surface gaps with suggested root causes.

Exportable audit trails satisfy finance close, SOC2 reviews, and internal governance without a manual screenshot hunt.

  • Change log: who edited filters, joins, or grain — with diff view
  • Pipeline attribution — "NRR dropped because stg_orders was 6h late"
  • Scheduled reconciliation jobs against billing and CRM systems
  • PDF/CSV export for audit committees and regulatory filings
  • Enterprise Brain traces "why did churn increase?" to model + metric changes
